Our Heritage

Three Generations of Excellence

For over six decades, the Moonlight Furniture legacy has been built on craftsmanship, integrity, and a commitment to quality

1st

The Visionary Founder

Haji Fazle Alam

Haji Fazle Alam founded Moonlight Furniture with a revolutionary belief: luxury should not be exclusive to the wealthy. With skilled hands and an entrepreneurial spirit, he began crafting furniture that combined timeless elegance with affordability.

His workshop in Bulandshahr became known for meticulous attention to detail and commitment to customer satisfaction. Every piece that left his workshop carried the promise of quality that would last generations.

Under his leadership, Moonlight Furniture established itself as a trusted local brand, known for handcrafted quality and personalized service that larger manufacturers couldn't match.

2nd

The Dedicated Builder

Mohammad Aslam inherited more than a businessâ€”he inherited a mission. Through decades of unwavering dedication, he transformed Moonlight Furniture from a local workshop into a trusted regional brand serving clients across Uttar Pradesh.

Under his leadership, the Moonlight Furniture expanded its reach while maintaining the founding principles of quality and affordability. His perseverance through challenging times ensured that his father's dream continued to flourish.

Mohammad Aslam pioneered the Moonlight Furniture's expansion into institutional furniture, securing contracts with schools, hospitals, and government offices while maintaining the personalized approach that defined the brand.
